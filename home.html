What is CancerKidsFirst GTA ?

Based in
Toronto

We are CancerKidsFirst GTA, a non-profit chapter running under the official CancerKidsFirst organization. Currently there are around 70 different chapters all over the world operating under the CancerKidsFirst name and you can find our chapter situated in the Greater Toronto Area.

We reach out to hospitals across the GTA but also try to reach patients all across the world who are battling cancer.
