Art Initiatives

General Arts Program:

Cards and Crafts

Our main program here at CancerKidsFirst GTA is the cards and crafts program. To participate you can to create uplifiting cards in either physical form like the ones shown in the attached picture or digital e-cards for pediatric cancer patients in Canada and around the world. Even if you are not from Canada, you may still participate and earn volunteer hours if they are applicable in your country.

General Arts Program:

Hospital Cards

For this Initiative and the cards can have any theme but they should not be addressed to a specific name Cards will be distributed amongst all patients at Grand River Hospital in Kitchener, Ontario.
Note: We are only accepting physical cards
